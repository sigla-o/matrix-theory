# Mathematical Tasks

This file contains a list of mathematical tasks arising from the logical map of Matrix Theory. The tasks are formulated as conceptual questions – they can be solved using mathematical tools based on the principles given in the map.

---

## 1. Derivation of the Pressure Imbalance

**Given:** Matrix point step d = 10⁻³⁵ m. Proton internal matrix step d_p = 10⁻⁵⁵ m. Number of parallel channels N = 10²⁰. Proton Qn layer number n_p ≈ 5.2 × 10¹⁹.

**Questions:**
- How to express the pressure asymmetry Δρ between the 0° and 180° half-phases caused by a single proton from these quantities?
- Why is n_p / N ≈ 0.52? Does this ratio have a deeper meaning?
- How does Δρ decrease with distance r from the proton? Why exactly 1/r²?

---

## 2. The Gravitational Constant G

**Given:** Background phase shift δ, ranging from 0 to N (= 10²⁰) depending on the local spherical curvature.

**Questions:**
- How to express G as the integral of Δρ over all space around a single proton?
- How does G depend on the total size R of the Universe?
- Is G completely constant at all scales, or should it be variable?

## 3. Proton Mass from Matrix Parameters

**Given:** Proton radius r_p ≈ 0.84 × 10⁻¹⁵ m. Number of points in the proton's internal matrix Qn, N_TE.

**Questions:**
- Can the proton mass m_p be expressed as a function of d, d_p, N, and the rotation period τ?
- What is the relationship between m_p and N? (In the map: n_p ≈ 4 × (m_P / m_p))
- How to express the Planck mass m_P from here?

## 4. The 10²⁰ Relationship and the Antimatter Threshold

**Given:** Vertical step 10²⁰. Phase shift between half-phases caused by spherical curvature. Experimentally determinable energy threshold at which antimatter begins to form.

**Questions:**
- How to connect these three quantities in one equation?
- Can the third be calculated from two known ones?
- What energy value does the theory predict for the antimatter threshold?

## 5. The Fine-Structure Constant

**Given:** N = 10²⁰. Number of half-phases = 2.

**Questions:**
- Can the fine-structure constant α ≈ 1/137 be related to N?
- For example, α ~ 1 / (N / something)? Or α ~ 4 / N?
- What are the possible models that derive α from N?

**Given:** Two-level modulations (10⁻⁵⁵ m and 10⁻³⁵ m). Transition from Strong force to Universe TE dominance with distance.

**Questions:**

- How to express the quantization of energy levels?
- What are the orbital shapes (s, p, d...) in the context of matrix symmetry?

## 7. Conditions for Nuclear Stability

**Given:** The nucleus as an imitation of the Universe. The number of neutrons determined by geometry and flow organization.

**Questions:**
- How to calculate the required number of neutrons N_n for nuclear stability from the number of protons Z?
- Why do "islands of stability" exist?
- How to express the radioactivity threshold?

## 8. The Uncertainty Principle as a Consequence of Channel Limitation

**Given:** N = 10²⁰ parallel channels. The electron is a two-half-phase closed sphere (size 10⁻⁷⁵ m × 10⁻⁵⁵ m) occupying a specific subset of channels. In each Q1 cycle, the flows use this subset to maintain the electron's internal organization. An observer, when making a measurement, restricts the number of available channels – to obtain information about position, the subset of channels through which the flow may pass must be reduced.

**Questions:**
- How to express the minimum quantum of action (Planck's constant h) from N and the size of the electron's channel subset?
- How does the form of the uncertainty principle Δx · Δp ≥ const arise from the number of channels?
- Why is the constant exactly ħ/2? Can it be derived from N and the matrix parameters?
