# Matrix Theory

## What is it?

Matrix Theory is a new, unified concept of physics that explains all fundamental interactions — gravity, strong force, electromagnetism, and weak force — through a single mechanism: **absolutely synchronous rotation of matrix points** and the dynamics of **two opposite TE flows**.

The theory replaces both General Relativity and quantum mechanics with a simple, visually imaginable model where space is a fixed cubic grid, time is absolute, and all matter consists of organized flow structures.

## Why does it matter?

- **Unification:** All four forces emerge from one principle.
- **Dark energy and dark matter:** Not separate mysteries — they are integral parts of the theory.
- **Absolute time and static space:** Bring clarity back to physics.
- **Life and consciousness:** Naturally included in the model.
