# Ka iesaistities / How to contribute

## Palidziba ir laipni gaidita sadas jomas:

- **Matematiska formalizacija:** Parverst konceptualos aprakstus precizos vienadojumos, izrekinat fizikalas konstantes no teorijas pamatprincipiem.
- **Eksperimentalo testu izstrade:** Piedavat veidus, ka parbaudit teorijas unikalas prognozes.
- **Tulkosana:** Palidzet tulkot materialus citas valodas.
- **Kludu labosana un diskusijas:** Atrast un zinot par logiskam kludam, pretrunam, neskaidribam.
- **Vizualie materiali:** Veidot animacijas, diagrammas, kas ilustre teorijas mehanismus.

## Noteikumi

- Saglabajiet cienpilnu toni.
- Visi argumenti lai balstas logika un/vai noverojamos datos.
- Teorijas autors patur tiesibas pienemt vai noraidit izmainas.

## Sazina

Izmantojiet `Issues` sadalu jautajumiem un diskusijam.

---

## Help is welcome in the following areas:

- **Mathematical formalization:** Transform conceptual descriptions into precise equations, calculate physical constants from the theory's fundamental principles.
- **Experimental test design:** Propose ways to verify the theory's unique predictions.
- **Translation:** Help translate materials into other languages.
- **Error correction and discussion:** Find and report logical errors, contradictions, and unclear passages.
- **Visual materials:** Create animations and diagrams illustrating the theory's mechanisms.

## Rules

- Maintain a respectful tone.
- All arguments should be based on logic and/or observable data.
- The theory's author reserves the right to accept or reject changes.

## Communication

Use the `Issues` section for questions and discussions.
