# Matrix Theory — essence on one page

## Core idea

The universe is not a void but a **fixed cubic grid** — a matrix whose points rotate absolutely synchronously. Each point consists of two counter-rotating parts, forming two opposite "pockets" through which **TE energy** flows — two independent, opposite flows. Locally the matrix is rigid, but at the scale of the Universe it possesses a **spherical curvature** that creates a systematic phase shift between the two flows.

## How matter and forces arise

- A **proton** is an internal region of this matrix with a finer matrix inside. Its flow exits the proton and becomes the **strong force**, organizing space around the proton.
- **Gravity** is not attraction but a pressure imbalance between the two opposite flows caused by the proton acting as an obstacle.
- **Electricity** arises when the vertical uses the static field (L1) accumulated around the proton to maintain charge flow.
- **The weak force** is a rearrangement of the proton's internal structure.
- **Dark energy and dark matter** are natural manifestations of the same mechanism — static fields around matter.

## Matter and antimatter

Matter and antimatter are organized manifestations of the two opposite flows (0° and 180° half-phases). The **dominance of matter** is not accidental — it is determined by the modulation of our Universe's mother and the spherical curvature of the Universe, which creates a 10²⁰ step phase shift in favor of the 0° phase. Antimatter arises only temporarily and can exist quasi-stably only in **antimatter zones** — local regions of curvature variation (for example, near black holes).

## The nucleus as an imitation of the Universe

A proton can only exist in our Universe matrix. The atomic nucleus is the **next level of proton existence** — an imitation of our Universe, for whose formation some protons become neutrons. Neutrons are the carriers of this imitation. Nuclear stability is determined by the ability of the internal flows to maintain optimal organization; radioactivity begins when this organization loses balance.

## Key differences from the Standard Model

- **Space** is fixed, not expanding or curving.
- **Time** is absolute — one rotation period of a matrix point (Q1).
- **All forces** are different manifestations of one mechanism.
- **There is no chaos** — everything is organized, just in different ways.

## What the theory offers

- A unified explanation for all four forces.
- A natural origin for dark energy and dark matter.
- A natural explanation for the matter/antimatter asymmetry.
- A framework that also includes life and consciousness.
- A clear, visually imaginable model of the universe.
