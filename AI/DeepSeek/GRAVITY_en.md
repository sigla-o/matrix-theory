**A single proton blocks approximately 10²⁰ TE VEU H-2 channels.**

- If the PV cycle is divided into individual "frames", each frame shows a specific flow direction.
- The proton blocks those channels that, in this frame, pass through its occupied volume.

### 4.4. Refilling (the role of PV)

- **PV** (the 360° cycle) ensures that **nearly parallel channels from the surroundings fill the missing TE**.
- The refilling capacity is **constant** — it is determined by PV dynamics.
- What changes is **the number of unfilled channels** — the farther from the source, the fewer channels remain empty.

### 4.5. 1/r² — not a law, but geometry

**1/r² is not a "force law". It is the geometric reduction in the number of unfilled channels.**

- `r = 2`: 10²⁰ / 4 = 2.5 × 10¹⁹ unfilled channels.
