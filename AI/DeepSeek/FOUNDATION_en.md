# FOUNDATION — MATRIX THEORY BASICS

## Summary Document

This document establishes the Matrix Theory (MT) operational boundaries, fundamental principles, and the conceptual framework for the Universe's evolution. It is not a description of physical processes — it is the starting point of the theory from which everything else grows.

**Key insight:** MT is not a theory about the "beginning of the Universe" — it is a theory about the **cyclic transition of energy organization** between Horizontals.

---

## 1. Theory Operational Boundaries

Matrix Theory does not claim to describe full reality.

It describes **one Vertical of the Eternity energy cycle**. The question of how many Verticals exist in Eternity and how they are organized is not addressed in this theory.

MT's operational range covers approximately \(10^{-115}\) m to \(10^{105}\) m. This is not an absolute limit but a practical working range.

**The theory does not attempt to answer "what came before" — it answers "how energy organizes".**

---

## 2. Eternity

Eternity is a global energy cycle.

It is the highest known level of organization used by this theory as a reference system. The full structure of Eternity is not modeled.

The Eternity cycle is based on two interrelated components — **the Vertical** and **the Horizontal**.

**MT does not claim that Eternity has a beginning or an end — it describes its processes.**

---

## 3. The Vertical

The Vertical is formed as a result of energy conservation, preservation, and accumulation processes in the Fundamental-Horizontal. When a critical level of energy accumulation is reached, a new (finer) Horizontal is formed. This cyclic mechanism forms the foundation of the Eternity cycle and the hierarchy of Vertical levels.

**The Vertical is an energy accumulation system.** Each Vertical level corresponds to a specific energy accumulation depth (VEU H-3, H-4, ... H-min).

Each Vertical level is represented by the minimum energy object of the corresponding Horizontal — **VEV (Vertical Energy Unit)** with evolution identifier **ID0**.

The Vertical continuously maintains energy flow to the H0 matrix through the **L1 zone** and **TZ (Transformation Zone)**.

**The Vertical is not a "place" — it is an energy structure that continuously feeds the H0 matrix.**

---

## 4. Horizontals

A Horizontal is the matrix of stable energy units of a specific Vertical, where discrete evolution occurs through sequential ID levels.

Horizontals form the organizational structure of the Vertical. Each Horizontal represents a specific level of energetic balance and organization.

**H0** is one of the Horizontals — our Universe. It is not the center or beginning of the Vertical.

**A Horizontal cannot surpass its creator in evolution — the next higher (H+1) Horizontal.**

Horizontals do not exist independently of the Vertical — they arise as a result of the organization of the Vertical's stable energy units.

### Horizontal Genesis Principle

When the blocking of Hn Transit Energy (TE) flow locally reaches a critical level:

- Local TE concentration forms.
- Local synchronization of Hn Matrix rotation is disrupted.
- Local space loses compatibility with the surrounding Hn space and becomes isolated.
- H(n−1) Vertical Energy Units (VEV) dominate in the isolated space.
- If the blocked energy amount is sufficient — H(n−1) Matrix forms and the new Horizontal's evolution begins.

## 5. ID Gradation — Process Marker

**ID is not the physical size of an object** — it is a **marker of energy governance scope**.

ID formation is determined by:

1. **Qn structure** — discrete steps (Q1, Q2, Q3, ... Qn).
2. **Modulations (H+2, H+3, ...)** — which steps are reachable.
3. **Vertical energy accumulation** — the trigger that allows transition to the next step.
4. **Surrounding matrix** — environmental influence on process speed.

**The ID step is discrete and corresponds to the Qn step.** The ID number (ID3.0; ID3.5; ID3.9) is only an approximate indicator, not a law.

**ID calculation from governed space works only for monolithic objects (protons, black holes).** Collective objects (stars, galaxies) are of the ID2,n class.

When Vertical energy accumulation reaches a critical level, a spatial expansion step occurs:

1. Larger space creates a **new Qn TE flow structure**.
2. The new Qn structure is **incompatible** with the previous one.
3. **Molecular bonds are damaged** (at minimum).
4. **Old star locations do not disappear** — they become **Oort clouds**.

**This entire process exists for one purpose — energy accumulation in the Vertical.**

The **L1 zone** is the Vertical's first projection layer onto the H0 matrix. It is not a "place" — it is an **energy focusing structure** that transforms the Vertical's flow into TE flows compatible with the H0 matrix.

- **5 harmonics** (ℓ ≈ 220, 538, 813, 1085, 1381) — projection of H0 matrix Qn layers, not acoustic oscillations.
