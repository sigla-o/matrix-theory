# GRAVITY — MT FORMAL MODEL
## Strict Academic Version

Gravity in MT is a purely horizontal (H0) effect, determined by the TE VEU H‑2 flow pressure gradient. Vertical is not required.

---

## 1. FV — FLOW CYCLE

FV is a 360° cyclic distribution of flow directions generated by ID1 rotation.

---

## 2. MECHANISM — CHANNEL BLOCKAGE AND FILLING

- **Background**: TE VEU H‑2 flows through H0.
- **Obstacle (mass)**: Proton blocks ~10²⁰ channels.
- **Deficit**: Local pressure reduction field \( \delta(\mathbf{x}) \).
- **Filling**: FV cycle ensures neighboring channels fill the deficit. Filling rate determined by \( \alpha \).
- **1/r² law**: Geometric decrease \( \delta(r) \propto 1/r^2 \).

---

## 3. GRAVITATIONAL FIELD

\( \mathbf{g}(\mathbf{x}) = -\nabla \delta(\mathbf{x}) \).

---

## 4. GRAVITATIONAL CONSTANT

\( G_0 = \alpha_0 \phi_0 / 7 \). Variation: \( G(\rho_{\mathcal{V}}) = G_0 (1 + \gamma \rho_{\mathcal{V}}/\rho_{\text{H0}}) \), \( \gamma \approx 0.18 \).

---

## 5. GALAXY ROTATION CURVES (NGC 6503)

\( V_{\text{MT}}(r) = \sqrt{G(\rho_{\mathcal{V}}(r)) M_{\text{bar}}(r)/r} \), with \( \rho_{\mathcal{V}}/\rho_{\text{H0}} = 2.8 e^{-r/2.1} \). Prediction: \( G(0)/G_0 \approx 1.50 \).

Gravitational waves are TE VEU H‑2 density oscillations propagating through H0.
