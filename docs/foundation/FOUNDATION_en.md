## 1. THEORY OPERATIONAL BOUNDARIES

Matrix Theory does not claim to describe the entirety of reality.

It describes **one Vertical of Eternity's energy cycle**. The question of how many Verticals exist in Eternity and how they are mutually organized is not addressed in this theory.

MT's operational range covers approximately \(10^{-115}\) m to \(10^{105}\) m. This is not an ultimate boundary but a practical working zone of the theory.

**The theory does not attempt to answer "what came before" — it answers "how energy organizes itself."**

### 1.1. MT's Relation to Classical Theories (GR, QED, QM, ΛCDM)

Matrix Theory **does not compete** with established physical theories and **does not attempt to disprove them**.

- Classical theories (Special and General Relativity, Quantum Electrodynamics, the Lambda-CDM model, quantum mechanics) are **empirical phenomenological laws** that excellently describe and predict observations within their domains (L1 and H0 levels).
- MT's role is to provide a **deeper mechanical origin** for these laws. If a classical theory successfully describes a regularity, MT does not challenge it — it shows how that regularity emerges from the TE transfer on the ID0 lattice, Qn structure, and Vertical (ID-1) interactions.

**"Map and territory" principle:**
- Classical physics is the accurate **map**.
- MT describes the **territory** (the discrete matrix and the Vertical) on which this map is built.

Thus, MT reduces to classical equations in the appropriate limits (see Correspondence Principle in MATHEMATICS, Chapter 7), but offers new explanations for anomalies (dark matter/energy) and predicts new effects (G variation, higher CMB peaks) not visible on the classical map.

## 4. BASIC DEFINITIONS

### 4.1. Eternity

Eternity is a global energy cycle.

It is the highest known level of organization, which this theory uses as a reference system. The full structure of Eternity is not modeled in the theory.

Eternity's cycle is based on two interrelated components — **Vertical** and **Horizontal**.

**MT does not claim that Eternity has a beginning or an end — it describes its processes.**

### 4.2. Vertical (ID-1)

The Vertical forms as a result of the conservation, preservation, and accumulation of energy from the Base Horizontal. When the critical energy accumulation level is reached, a new (finer) Horizontal is formed. This cyclic mechanism forms the basis of Eternity's cycle and the hierarchy of Vertical levels.

**The Vertical is an energy accumulation system.** Each Vertical level corresponds to a specific energy accumulation depth (VEU H-3, H-4, ... H-min), which in the ID system corresponds to **ID-1.n**.

**Formally:** The Vertical is a set of energy levels:
\[

\]
with total energy \( E_{\mathcal{V}} = \sum_{k=3}^{\text{min}} E_{H-k} \).

The Vertical continuously maintains energy flow to the H0 matrix through the **L1 zone** and **TZ (Transformation Zone)**.

**The Vertical is not a "place" — it is an energy structure that continuously feeds the H0 matrix.**

### 4.3. Horizontals (ID0 — ID5)

A Horizontal is a matrix of stable energy units of a specific Vertical, where discrete evolution occurs through successive ID levels.

**Formally:** Horizontal \( H_n \) is a lattice \( \mathcal{L}_n \) with Qn structure and TE flow field \( \Phi_n \).

**H0** is one of the Horizontals — our Universe. It is not the center or the beginning of the Vertical.

**A Horizontal cannot outpace its creator in evolution — the next higher (H+1) Horizontal.**

The H0 matrix in the ID system corresponds to **ID0**. All objects in the H0 matrix are classified by ID levels based on their size and modulation influence.
